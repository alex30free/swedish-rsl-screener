Market Climate Phases

🟢 Bull Market

RSL above 1.0 and SMA rising. The strongest phase — global markets in a broad uptrend, momentum is expanding. Ideal for full equity exposure.

🔵 Recovery

RSL below 1.0 but SMA turning upward. Markets recovering from a downturn. Early signal to begin building positions cautiously.

🟠 Consolidation

RSL above 1.0 but SMA flattening or declining. Bull market losing momentum. Consolidation or correction likely. Reduce risk, tighten stops.

🔴 Bear Market

RSL below 1.0 and SMA declining. Broad global downtrend. Defensive positioning, cash, or hedges are appropriate. Avoid new long exposure.

Methodology

RSL Calculation

Each index's daily closing price divided by its own 130-day (26-week) simple moving average. Values above 1.0 indicate the market is in an uptrend relative to its own 6-month history.

Trend SMA Overlay

A moving average applied to the RSL values themselves — not the price. This smooths out daily noise to reveal the underlying trend direction of global market momentum. Default: SMA-38 (38-day SMA of RSL).

Equal-weight average line

All 23 indices are averaged with equal weight to produce a single "global market climate" line. This produces a single number capturing worldwide equity momentum.

Data source

All price history fetched live from Yahoo Finance in your browser. 5 years of daily data, no server required. Reload to refresh. Indices may show slight delays depending on exchange hours.
